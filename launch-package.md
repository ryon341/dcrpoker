## Long Description

### Hook

DCR Poker is the fastest way to sharpen your preflop poker decisions. Built by the team behind Deer Creek Road custom poker tables, this training app puts you through real hand scenarios — tap Yes or No and find out immediately if you made the right call.

No fluff. No tutorials. Just reps.

---

### Core Features

**Level-Based Challenge Mode**
Work your way through 25 progressive levels of head-up preflop scenarios. Each level raises the stakes and complexity. Earn enough points to advance, earn bonus spins, and unlock new titles as you climb.

**GTO-Style Yes / No Decisions**
Every hand presents a straightforward call: Should the hero continue with this hand? You answer yes or no, then watch the full runout — the flop, turn, and river — and see the outcome. Instant feedback. No second-guessing.

**Random Runouts & Real Scoring**
The board runs out differently every time. Your score depends on both your decision accuracy *and* the hand outcome, just like at a real table. Correct plays get rewarded. Incorrect plays cost you — but never as much as folding equity at a live table.

**Daily Challenge Mode**
Five fresh hands every day, seeded to the calendar date so every player gets the same challenge. Complete the daily set, maintain your streak, and earn badges as you build a consistent study habit.

**Titles, Streaks & Stats**
Unlock poker titles as you level up — from *The Calling Station* at Level 1 to titles that mark real mastery. Track your accuracy, win rate, best streaks, and lifetime hands played on your personal stats dashboard.

**No Login Required to Start**
Jump straight into the action as a guest through Level 5. Sign up any time to save your progress permanently and unlock all levels — your progress carries over automatically.

---

### Why DCR Poker Helps

Poker decisions happen fast. The players who win consistently aren't just lucky — they've seen enough hands that the right move feels automatic.

DCR Poker is built on that principle: **repetition builds recognition**. Short sessions (under 5 minutes) are enough to run through a full challenge, review your reasoning, and log another day on your streak. Over time, the patterns stick.

Whether you're preparing for your first home game or sharpening your edge for regular sessions, DCR Poker gives you a structured way to train without stakes.
